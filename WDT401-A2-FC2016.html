F-Camp 2016

Ignite the light
A greeting, a gift from the above-mentioned students for the new students.
F-camp is an annual program held at FPT University. This year, towards the topic of CONNECTIVITY, F-Camp will give new students the opportunity to learn to get acquainted with FPT culture and exchange meetings between students. At F-Camp, you will:
Have a day to immerse yourself in a cheerful, active and expressive atmosphere.
Together, sing, dance, and “dance” together.
Listen to sincere sharing, learning experiences and upcoming student life.
